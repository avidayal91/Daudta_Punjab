ABOUT DAUDTA PUNJAB

Daudta Punjab is a movement dedicated to the People India, a movement to introduce sports & running as an activity for the youth to keep them engaged in lifestyles which will encourage improved health and wellness.

Daudta Punjab stands for the Punjabi ethos of fighting for what is right, fighting to overcome, fighting to get to the top and never give up!

Daudta Punjab comprises of an extremely competent and experienced team who have successfully delivered unparalleled running experiences to over 50,000 runners across the region in their large and small scale events.

The team comprises a mix of experienced professionals, prominent marathon organisers and runners of international standing.
